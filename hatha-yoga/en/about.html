About

Setsuko Hata

Sadhguru Certified Hatha Yoga Teacher

Authentic Hatha Yoga instruction by one of only four Sadhguru Certified Hatha Yoga Teachers in Japan, with over 1,750 hours of intensive residential training in India, offering both traditional Hatha Yoga for individuals and corporate wellness programs for organizations.

Mission

The purpose of yoga is to open up life's fullest possibility. Bringing body and mind into a state you can direct at will: that is where instruction begins.

Philosophy

Yoga is often mistaken for a stretching exercise. Its real meaning is union: the exchange between one's own life and the vastness beyond it.
