Frequently asked questions

Can I join without any yoga experience?+

Yes. Upa Yoga is designed as an entry point, and instruction is adapted to each person's condition.

What should I bring?+

Comfortable clothing and a yoga mat. Mat rental may be available depending on the venue.

How is this different from fitness yoga?+

Fitness yoga centers on physical exercise. Hatha Yoga works from an understanding of the body's mechanics toward a fuller practice that includes mind and energy.

Tell me about the corporate program.+

Programs are customized for organizations pursuing health management, in formats practical for the office. Contact us for details.
