About Setsuko

A Teacher.
A Vocalist.
A Seeker.

"I teach from the place where breath becomes music, and music returns to silence."

Setsuko Hata is a certified Hatha Yoga teacher and jazz vocalist based in Tokyo, weaving together decades of practice in contemplative movement and improvisational music. Her classes and performances invite practitioners to arrive fully — body, voice, and spirit.
